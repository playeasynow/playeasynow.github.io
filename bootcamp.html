— UT Austin Full-stack Bootcamp

Though working in software development management for 4 years taught me a lot about the overall implementation of features, I did not have the capacity to dive deep into development itself as a discipline. I decided to invest in myself and learn the full-stack via UT Austin's Coding Bootcamp. Not only has it grown my fascination and curiosity in the possibility of software and data management, it has reminded me how important a continuing education is.
