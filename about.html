— About

Welcome!

I’m currently working on my full stack development certification via the University of Texas Coding Bootcamp in Houston, TX.

My mother is Honduran and my father Colombian, so that provides me the gift of North, Central and South American roots. Because of my family's entrepreneurial outlook on life, I quickly understood the complexity of globalization, economic growth and migration at an early age which inspired a life-long journey of problem solving. I constantly try to expand and evolve my hard and soft skills, and apply them to equally varied methods of problem solving and understanding. When solving problems, I'm interested in understanding the social implications of the designed solutions while still considering the needs of the market and value to the enterprise.

The variety of my experience, from music performance to project management and software development, has allowed me to contribute to work for a diverse set of brands and organizations such as HP, Microsoft, New Relic and The Trevor Project. The past 4 years as a management consultant advanced my career significantly, as I traveled the country and abroad to clients tackling complex problems of scale and social impact.

Lately, I have been developing software ranging from HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript to React.js, Node.js, MongoDB and C#. And more! The bootcamp has been both a sabbatical from a fast-paced career, and a chance to take a step back and invest in my own skills.

In my spare time I like to listen to podcasts and books, sing, play the piano and violin, and ride my bike.

My bootcamp ends February 2019, and I am looking for positions as a Product Manager or Full Stack Developer.
