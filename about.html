— About

Welcome!

I’m a technical program manager currently working on my full-stack development certification via the UT Austin Houston Coding Bootcamp.

I'm constantly trying to expand and evolve my set of technical + soft skills and apply them to equally varied methods of problem solving and understanding. I believe that clever solutions to both high and low level problems can be found through combinations of these processes and ideas.

When solving problems, I'm interested in understanding the social implications of the designed solutions while still considering the needs of the market and value to the enterprise.

The variety of my experience, from music performance to program management and software development, has allowed me to contribute to work for a diverse set of brands and organizations such as HP, Microsoft, Google, The Trevor Project, Twitter and my ever-evolving community. The past 4 years as a consultant brought me to new heights, as I traveled the country and abroad to clients tackling complex problems of scale, globalization, and social impact.

Recently, I've been working on my front and back-end development skills ranging from HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript to React.js, Node.js, MongoDB and C#. And more! This bootcamp has been both a break from a fast-paced career, and a chance to take a step back and invest in my own skills.

In my spare time I like to listen to podcasts and books, sing, play the piano and violin, and ride my bike.

My bootcamp ends February 2019, and I am looking for positions as a Technical PM, Product Manager or Front-End Developer.
