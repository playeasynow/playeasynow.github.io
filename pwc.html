about

— PwC

Out of undergrad, I moved to San Francisco and started my professional career with PwC (PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP). I studied Marketing in school and I knew I wanted to work in the technology industry, so I asked the firm to place me on projects that were relevant and challenging. As a management consultant, I often worked with business and technical teams to deliver services in e-commerce, marketing and sales operations, and even risk management.


↓ See below for highlights of my experience

BXT at PwC
2018

Location

Miami, FL

Timeline

January to July 2018

BXT Curriculum Designer and Co-Lead

BXT stands for business, experience and technology. PwC began a firmwide initiative in 2017 to bring design thinking, agile practices, and analytics to staff, while modernizing our delivery approach by building cross-functional teams and tackling broader scope in our services. On a tour of duty, I joined the team to contribute my change management and design thinking experience.

  • BXT Business Development Curriculum
    • Aligned existing internal business development teams around a new curriculum and training program
    • Achieved signoff on launch, and began training for over 500 senior staff in the US, UK, and Australia
  • Agile and Design Thinking
    • Co-led design sessions in NYC, Miami and Chicago to gather feedback from practice leaders across the firm
    • Designed guidance for all staff levels to adopt Agile and Design Thinking practices
    • Secured approval of guidance and began distributing material to small focus groups for more feedback

Salesforce & Zuora Implementation
2017

Location

San Francisco, CA

Timeline

February to December 2017

Training and Business Process Lead

As part of the team implementing Zuora billing software and managing integrations with Salesforce and the product platform, I was responsible for ensuring our client's sales force (>500) adopted the newly designed lead-to-revenue process through training and communications. In tandem, I learned about feature development, data storage and migration, and fundamental financial and accounting processes.

  • Business Process Design
    • Co-facilitated design sessions with finance, sales ops, product, business apps and sales teams
    • Focused scope on 8 use cases (trial, new customer, repeat, cancelation, upgrade, downgrade), over 1,500 user stories and >500 users.
  • Training Curriculum and Planning
    • Planned user acceptance testing, super-user training, and end-user training
    • Led a 2-person team developing an over 100 page training curriculum (yikes!) that new training would be sourced from
    • Transitioned training program to client's instructional team and technical teams
