about

— PwC

Out of undergrad, I moved to San Francisco and started my professional career with PwC (PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP). I studied Marketing and knew I wanted to work in the technology industry, so I interviewed for projects that were relevant and challenging. As a management consultant, I often worked with business and technical teams to deliver services in ecommerce, marketing and sales operations, training and even risk management.

Internal program

Miami, FL

Timeline

BXT Curriculum Designer and Co-Lead

On a tour of duty, I joined the team to contribute my change management and design thinking experience.

    • Secured approval of guidance and began distributing material to small focus groups for more feedback

Client project

Oracle Configure-Price-Quote Implementation
Client Project

Location

Chicago, IL

Timeline

April 2016 to January 2017

CPQ Implementation Project Management

Configure-Price-Quote software helps sales teams, channels and ecommerce sites automate the sales cycle and sell faster and more easily. As part of a 3-person project management team responsible for a $3M implementation, we led the business, technical and product teams through the adoption of the new software. From requirements definition through user acceptance testing and go-live, we helped our client achieve shorter sales cycles, closing deals quicker and encouraging renewals.

  • Business and Technical Team Management
    • Aligned business and technical stakeholders on MVP scope along a 6-sprint plan
    • Though the largest project in scope in recent years for the client, we took them from developing to testing and helped plan cutover activities in preparation for go-live
  • Target Operating Model
    • Managed a 3-person workstream defining a future state target operating model for the sales and marketing organizations
    • Successfully held over 30 sessions to detail day-to-day activities of various staff, to ensure processes were not redundant and ultimately aligned to the leadership's company-wide strategy
