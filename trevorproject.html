— The Trevor Project

The Trevor Project's mission is to end suicide in LGBTQ youth through crisis and intervention services, and I had the privilege to uphold this mission in San Francisco. As Ambassador Chair, I was responsible for leading a group of 15 volunteers, as well as, the designing, planning and executing of our year-round events in the community and with corporate partners. One of my favorite annual events was Trevor Runway, a fundraiser and irreverant evening to remind attendees that self-love is radical and nourishing.

Through my work over 3 years, we built partnerships with Twitter, Google, Safeway and Salesforce, amongst other supportive companies. I could not be more proud of the funds raised, but even more, about the presence and awareness established.

Continuing Involvement

Though I've moved and am back in Texas, I continue to support The Trevor Project in any way that I can. We're currently evaluating the establishment of a volunteer group in Austin to increase awareness and ensure all youth in central Texas have access to its life-saving services.
