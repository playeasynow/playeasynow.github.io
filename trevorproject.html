— The Trevor Project

I had the privilege to uphold The Trevor Project's mission in San Francisco. As Ambassador Chair, I was responsible for leading our group of 15 volunteers, as well as, the desiging, planning and executing of our year-round events in the community and with corporate partners. One of my favorite annual events was Trevor Runway, a fundraiser and irreverant evening to remind people to love themselves above all else.

Through my work over 3 years, we built partnerships with Twitter, Google, Safeway and Salesforce, amongst other supportive companies. I could not be more proud of the awareness we raised.
